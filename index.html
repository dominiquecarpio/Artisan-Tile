How We Work

Our 5-step process

01
Free Consultation

We come to your home, assess the space, discuss your vision, and walk you through material options at no charge.

02
Design & Estimate

We create a detailed layout plan with material recommendations and provide a transparent itemized quote.

03
Material Selection

Our Story

Built on craft.
Driven by pride.

Artisan Tile & Stone Specialists LLC was founded in 2006 by a single craftsman with a simple belief: tile work is an art form — not a commodity. Over 18 years, we've grown into a team of certified installers who share that same obsession with quality.

We're based in the Detroit Metro Area and serve residential and commercial clients across Michigan. Every project — from a single bathroom to a full commercial renovation — gets our full attention and our name on it.

Michigan Licensed & Bonded

Fully licensed for all residential and commercial tile and masonry work in Michigan.

TCNA Certified Methods

Our installations follow TCNA standards — the gold standard in the tile industry.

Written Workmanship Warranty

Every installation comes with a written warranty covering workmanship defects.
